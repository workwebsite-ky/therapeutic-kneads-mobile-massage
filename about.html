About Eve

The greatest wealth is health — and my mission is to help you feel your very best, wherever you are.

My story

Healing has always been my calling

I'm Eve, a licensed massage therapist and the founder of Therapeutic Kneads Mobile Massage Services. What began as a passion for helping people feel better in their own bodies grew into a full mobile practice serving Houston and the surrounding areas.

Earning my license was a turning point — it let me bring a higher standard of care to every client, whether I'm working out chronic tension one-on-one or setting up at a busy corporate wellness event. I believe massage isn't a luxury add-on; it's real, therapeutic care for a body that works hard every day.

Every session I offer is professional, unhurried and completely tailored to you. That's the difference my clients feel — and the reason so many of them come back.
